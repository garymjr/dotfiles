Analyze the following prompt and enhance it for clarity, specificity, and effectiveness:

$ARGUMENTS

First, evaluate if the prompt's intent is clear and specific. If the intent is unclear, ambiguous, or lacks sufficient detail, ask clarifying questions such as:

- What specific outcome are you trying to achieve?
- Who is the target audience for this prompt?
- What context or background information is missing?
- Are there any constraints or requirements I should know about?
- What format or style do you prefer for the response?

If the intent is clear, enhance the prompt by:

1. Adding specific context and background information
2. Clarifying the desired output format and structure
3. Including relevant constraints or guidelines
4. Improving the sequence and flow of information
5. Adding examples or templates where helpful
6. Ensuring the prompt is actionable and specific

Provide only the enhanced version of the prompt.
